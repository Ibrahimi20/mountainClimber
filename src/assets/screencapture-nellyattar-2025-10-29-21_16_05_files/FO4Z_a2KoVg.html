Welcome to the world of Dream, Girl — hosted by Dr. Sheen Gurrib. I’m a Cambridge PhD, Oxford grad, ex-consultant, and now award-nominated podcaster on a mission to help women become powerful, confident, and unstoppable. Here, you’ll find unfiltered conversations with iconic women across the world — actors, founders, influencers, creators — all sharing what it really takes to succeed. We talk career highs, heartbreaks, identity, ambition, money, and what it means to be that girl in a world that wasn’t built for us. If you’re a woman chasing big dreams (or just figuring it out), this is your space. New episodes of Dream, Girl every Monday — plus raw, real content in between.
